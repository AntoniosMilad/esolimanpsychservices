Bethany Valenti

Bethany Valenti is an AMFT #136848 intern working directly under the supervision of Dr. Erene Soliman. She has a Multiple-Subject Teaching Credential, a Bachelor’s Degree in Communicative Disorders, a Master’s Degree in Education with an emphasis on Best Practices, and a Master’s in Counseling with the intention of becoming a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist. Bethany has worked in the school system for the past 16 years as a classroom teacher, a mentor teacher and student success coach, and an intervention support provider working extensively with behavioral health services to write treatment plans and implement various strategies within the classroom for both behavioral and mental health needs. She aspires to one day provide mental health services to educators and support staff within the school system. She believes in using an integrative approach to fit each individual’s needs in their present state and incorporates a variety of methods including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Solution Focused Therapy, Strength Based Therapy, and places emphasis on theories such as Systemic Theory, and Bowen’s Family System. Bethany appreciates diversity and has a deepening understanding of how systems impact a person’s overall wellness. She works hard to create a healthy therapeutic environment free from judgment where her clients can express themselves freely.

Roger Zaldivar

About Us

Dr. Soliman's Psychological Practice is aimed at providing high-quality psychological services and counseling. Pursuing psychological treatment can be initially an intimidating process; however, as with every new process, therapy can be an invaluable learning experience to help with managing anxiety, depression, anger, and stress, in addition to dealing with substance abuse issues. Therapy can also help with skill building in the area of interpersonal communications, as well as with conflict resolution. Diversity variables are also an important aspect of treatment, as well as medical issues that may be impacting optimal psychological health.
