Psychotherapy

At Cultivate Culturally Competent Clinicians, Inc., we offer comprehensive Psychotherapy services dedicated to supporting the mental health and emotional well-being of children, adolescents, and adults.. Our therapists specialize in providing individualized care tailored to each personâ€™s unique needs, fostering a safe and nurturing environment for healing and growth.

Our Psychotherapy services include one-on-one sessions where children, teenagers, and adults can explore their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors in a confidential and supportive setting. We utilize evidence-based therapeutic approaches such as culturally informed Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR), Play Therapy, and Art Therapy to help clients develop healthy coping mechanisms, improve self-esteem, and address issues such as anxiety, depression, trauma, and behavioral challenges.

In addition to individual therapy, we offer group therapy sessions that allow children to connect with peers facing similar challenges, providing a sense of community and mutual support. Family therapy is also available to address systemic issues within the family unit, enhance communication, and strengthen relationships.

Understanding the critical role of parents and caregivers, we provide resources and guidance to help them support their children's mental health journey. Our goal is to empower children and their families with the tools and strategies needed to navigate life's challenges and achieve long-term emotional well-being.

At Cultivate Culturally Competent Clinicians, Inc., we are committed to creating a compassionate, inclusive, and accessible space where every child, teenager, and adult can thrive. All of our clinicians are trained on the Addressing Framework model by Pamela Hays and respond with cultural empathy and understanding. Our Psychotherapy services are designed to make a meaningful impact, fostering resilience and promoting positive mental health outcomes for all children in our community.



Psychodiagnostic evaluations

We also offer comprehensive Psychodiagnostic evaluations to rule out ADHD, Autism, or to help with differential diagnosing. Our psychodiagnostic evaluations include a clinical interview, 2-4 hours of psychological testing, feedback session, and a comprehensive report with tailored recommendations to the referral need. Our clinicians conduct thorough assessments to diagnose mental health conditions, identify strengths and challenges, and provide personalized recommendations. These evaluations are essential for identifying developmental challenges and opportunities, developing effective treatment plans, guiding therapy, and improving overall well-being. Committed to fostering mental health and resilience, we provide a compassionate and professional approach to meet the unique needs of every client.

About Us

Dr. Soliman's Psychological Practice is aimed at providing high-quality psychological services and counseling. Pursuing psychological treatment can be initially an intimidating process; however, as with every new process, therapy can be an invaluable learning experience to help with managing anxiety, depression, anger, and stress, in addition to dealing with substance abuse issues. Therapy can also help with skill building in the area of interpersonal communications, as well as with conflict resolution. Diversity variables are also an important aspect of treatment, as well as medical issues that may be impacting optimal psychological health.
